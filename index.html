JavaScript Coding Quiz Challenge

Directions of the game:

  1. Start button will initiate the timer and present you with a series of questions pertaining to JavaScript.
  2. Each question will be multiple choice.
  3. If your answer is wrong, time will be subtracted from the timer and you will be notified it was incorrect
  4. If you are correct, no time will be deducted and you will get the next question
  5. Once the timer ends OR you have answered all the questions, You will be given a score.
  6. At the end of the game you will be able to record your initials with your highScore.
